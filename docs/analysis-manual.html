Club Intelligence

The differentiator is analysis depth, not skin.

modDJs is designed to decide whether one section can be woven into another. That requires more than track-level BPM and key. The engine now reasons at phrase level.

Phrase fingerprints Tonal stability Texture motion Recall score Kick conflict Vocal density

What is fingerprinted per phrase

  • Local BPM hint and rhythmic confidence
  • Key hint and tonal stability
  • Energy, vocal density, instrumental ratio
  • Transition friendliness and texture score
  • Spectral fingerprint and timbral signature
  • Club recall score for re-entry or handoff use

How modDJs decides that a slice is worth reusing

  1. Find phrase markers with stable rhythm and enough section identity.
  2. Score tonal lock so recall slices do not destabilize harmonic flow.
  3. Estimate texture motion and energy contour to avoid dead repeats.
  4. Use transition friendliness and instrumental space to rank handoff candidates.
  5. Combine this into a club recall score per phrase, not just per track.

This is what enables patterns like minute 1 from one source, bar-tight bridge from another, and later re-entry from an earlier phrase without it sounding arbitrary.
